Privacy Policy

How CareInMyCity collects, uses, and protects information.

♡ We use information to improve user experience, respond to messages, and help families organize resources more effectively.
1

Information We Collect

CareInMyCity may collect information you provide when you fill out forms, create or use My Care Folder tools, subscribe to updates, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, and any details you choose to share with us.

2

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our website, personalize your experience, respond to your messages, and help you organize and manage care-related resources.

3

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve functionality and content. You can adjust cookie preferences in your browser settings.

4

Carl and My Care Folder

When you use Carl or My Care Folder tools, we may store the information you enter to help you create, organize, and access your care plans and resources.

5

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our website and deliver our services, and only as necessary to perform those functions on our behalf.

6

Your Choices

You can choose not to provide certain information, though some features may be limited. You may also contact us to access, update, or request deletion of your information at any time.

7

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at hello@elitemediagroup.io.
