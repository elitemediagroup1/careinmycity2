CareInMyCity was not built as a business exercise. It was built from lived experience. Charlie Brugnolotti has spent two decades in marketing, but the reason this platform exists is more personal than professional. As a caregiver for a parent with dementia and a father to a daughter with severe autism, Charlie has lived the confusion, urgency, paperwork, and emotional weight that families face when they are trying to make care decisions. He built CareInMyCity to make that search clearer, calmer, and more useful for other families. The goal is simple: help people understand local options, organize next steps, and feel less alone in the process.

What CareInMyCity Tries to Do

⌖

Explain local options

Clear, plain-language information about care and support services in your community.

☑

Organize the next step

Tools and resources to help you compare, plan, and move forward with confidence.

♡

Support family decisions

Guidance for the real conversations that help families make care decisions together.
